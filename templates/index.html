New Verification System

Project Overview

Implemented a new verification system for retail customers that significantly improved order processing efficiency, improved fraud prevention, and allowed more orders to be placed.

Challenge

The existing verification process was manual, time-consuming, and prone to errors; leading to increased chargeback activity and lost revenue.

Solution
  • Designed and implemented automated verification workflows
  • Have verification happen before Credit Card authorization to reduce authorization charges.
  • Send 3rd part all order related information to help learn and grow with the customer base.
  • Established monitoring and alerting systems for when issues occur.
Results
  • Increased orders by $15 million through improved processing efficiency
  • Reduced fraud by 95% through new verification process.
  • Verification team reduced from 11 to 1.
  • Manual verification work reduced from over 500 orders daily to 15.
New Surcharging Product

Project Overview

Developed and launched a new surcharging product for business customers that successfully recouped lost processing fees and improved revenue streams.

Challenge

Business customers were using Credit Cards to pay for their orders, which was costing the company over $6 million a year in just processing fees.

Solution
  • Integrated with 3rd party surcharging provider to allow business customers to pay with their Credit Card.
  • Created new messaging and flow for customers using a Credit Card to pay for their order.
  • Built integrations with Finance and Accounting to allow for the proper recording of the surcharge.
Results
  • Recouped $5 million of the $6 million in lost processing fees
  • Increased debit card usage by 10+%.
  • Established a scalable model for future fee structures, future legal requirements, and future surcharging.
Report Revamp and Roadmap

Project Overview

Completely revamped the reporting infrastructure and created a comprehensive roadmap for future reporting initiatives, significantly improving data accessibility and decision-making capabilities.

Challenge

Existing reports were outdated, inconsistent, and required significant manual effort to maintain, leading to inefficient decision-making and wasted resources. Any lapse in reporting could cost them accreditation and lost clients.

Solution
  • Conducted comprehensive audit of existing 120+ reports
  • Built out 70+ reports for automated data refresh and distribution systems
  • Transformed parts of the data warehouse to have more accurate data
  • Developed comprehensive roadmap for future reporting enhancements
  • Established best practices and documentation for report maintenance/creation
Results
  • Recreated and optimized 70+ reports for better usability
  • Saved 20+ hours per month through automation and standardization
  • Improved data accuracy and consistency across all reports
  • Enhanced stakeholder satisfaction with timely, relevant insights
  • Allowed company to keep accreditation and keep clients happy.
